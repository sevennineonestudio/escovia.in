About Escovia

We started with one houseboat and a long drive to Munnar.

Escovia — began as a small group of friends planning Kerala trips for each other. It's since grown into a studio of stays, itineraries, and a community, but the way we work hasn't changed much.

What we believe

Fewer stays, known well

We'd rather list fifty stays we've actually slept in than five hundred we've only seen in photographs. Every recommendation on Escovia comes from someone who has been there.

How we plan

Around you, not a template

No fixed packages. Every itinerary starts from your dates and your pace, and gets built stay by stay, drive by drive, around that.

Why the community

The map is never finished

Travellers keep finding places we haven't — a trail, a village, a quiet stretch of coast. The community exists so that knowledge doesn't disappear after one trip.
